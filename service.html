Nearly a century ago, we started providing logistics services as a barge service company in the ports of Tokyo and Yokohama. Today, we are a turnkey logistics supplier, offering a wide array of logistics solutions for harbor transportation, land transportation, warehousing, maritime transportation, and international freight forwarding.
As a total logistics company, we deploy our "Three Powers", namely "Power of Transportation", "Power of Terminal Operation", and "Power of Total Logistics" that provide sophisticated logistics services including 3rd Party Logistics (3PL) and Supply Chain Management (SCM) to meet the needs of the clients, while contributing to the realization of a recyclable society by promoting tender logistics for the Earth, particularly reverse logistics.
