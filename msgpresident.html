We are committed to our corporate philosophy of "becoming a total logistics company that provides optimal logistics services to our clients, thereby contributing to society and cultivating our corporate value." In today's changing business environment where social needs are more and more diversified, we will support our clients' business activities by developing and utilizing a Logistics System that comprehensively and efficiently integrates various modes of transportation via land, marine, and air.
We will strive to merit the unqualified trust of stakeholders, including our clients. I thus ask you to provide us with your further valued support.

  2. Viewing customer satisfaction as No.1, we will provide safe, secure and speedy logistics services.
  3. We will follow the law and conduct business activities in a fair and honest way.
